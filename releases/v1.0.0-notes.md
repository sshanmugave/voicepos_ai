# Smart Billing & Sales Management App - v1.0.0

Initial public release for hackathon demo.

## Highlights

- Offline-first billing and sales management app
- Multilingual first-launch setup (Tamil, English, Hindi, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada)
- Login/register with local session persistence
- Business-type-specific setup and templates:
  - Tea Shop
  - Restaurant
  - Salon / Barber Shop
  - Juice Shop
  - Bakery
  - Street Vendor
- Fast billing screen with quick item tap and quantity management
- Payment options: Cash, UPI QR, Card, Credit
- Direct WhatsApp bill sharing to entered customer number
- Dashboard with sales metrics and insights
- Sales analytics view with daily trend and top items
- AI insights and stock-reorder suggestions
- B2B marketplace with supplier products and order history
- Notifications center for alerts and order updates
- Feedback page to collect user improvement suggestions
- Export support for PDF/CSV

## Included Downloadable Artifact

- `releases/app-release.apk`
- `releases/app-release.apk.sha1`

## Build

```bash
flutter pub get
flutter build apk --release
```
